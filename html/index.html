Coaches information...

Rochell Hill - Head Coach XC & Track/Throws

Coach Hill is in her 3rd year at SPHS and has over 15 years of high school coaching experience. Prior to coaching, she competed in Cross-Country and Track at Northwest Missouri State University. Coach Hill teaches in the science department at South Paulding High School.

Christian Munoz - Head Coach XC & Track/Distance

Coach Munoz is in his 2nd year at SPHS. He has previously coached baseball at the Jr. Spartan Track program. Prior to coaching he competed at Florida in Cross-Country.

Joseph Scott - Jumps

Coach Scott has taught at SPHS for 9 years. Coach Scott has coached Track (5 years) and Football (defensive coordinator) as well as teaching in the science department at SPHS. He graduated from Georgia Southern University and is married with 3 children.

Mike Wagner - Pole Vault

Coach Wagner has coached the vaulters at South for ?? years. He also coaches football and teaches in the physical education department at South. Coach Wagner is a graduate of ???? University.

Kevin Robinson - Sprints

Coach Robinson is in his 8th year coaching at SPHS. Coach Robinson competed internationally for his native country, U.S. Virgin Islands. He was part of the USVI 4 x 100 relay team that won a bronze medal at the 1991 Pan Am Games. He competed at the collegiate level for the University of Arkansas.
